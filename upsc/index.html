Mains Descriptive PYQs — Reference Library

878 Mains questions — GS-I, GS-II, GS-III, GS-IV and Essay, spanning 2013–2025 — searchable and filterable by paper, subject, subtopic and year. Mains answers are descriptive and scored subjectively, so this is a read-only reference — not a graded quiz. Practice by writing your own answers in a timed session, then compare notes.

Coverage: GS-I/II/III questions for 2013–2025 (upstream dataset + OCR'd from official PDFs); GS-IV and Essay for 2022–2025 (older years and Optional papers are not yet ingested). Subject/subtopic tags are auto-assigned from question text and may be imperfect — see the official PDFs on upsc.gov.in.
